About Us

PICT FOSS Club was established on < Date > seeing the interest of numerous students towards FOSS and related events. The purpose of this club is to provide the members with more exposure to Open Source Softwares and encourage collaboration to work on various projects developed in the club.

Objectives:

  1. Educate and encourage students to use FOSS to the fullest extent.
  2. Organise special workshops/club meets/online sessions for students/faculty.
  3. Promote use of FOSS at college/university level by organising special workshops/online sessions for outside students.
